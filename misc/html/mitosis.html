Mitosis

The process where a single cell divides into two identical daughter cells.

Interphase

  • Cell grows
  • Prepares for mitosis
  • Replicates DNA
  • Has 3 stages

  • G1: make new organelles
  • S: copy DNA & Chromosmes
  • G2: prepare to divide

Prophase

  • Chromosomes condense

  • Nuclear membrane disappears

Metaphase

  • Chromosomes line up in the
    middle

  • Each sister chromatid
    is facing one of
    the ends of the cell

Anaphase

  • Sister chromatids separate
    from each other

  • Each sister chromatid
    moves to one end
    of the cell

Telophase

  • Forms new nuclear membrane
    around each cluster of
    separated chromosomes

  • Chromosomes might turn
    into chromatids
    (less condensed DNA)
